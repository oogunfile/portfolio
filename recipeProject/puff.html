Puff-Puff

A popular Nigerian deep-fried dough snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and mix until a smooth, sticky dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and let the dough rise for 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or pan over medium heat.
  5. Scoop small portions of the dough into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on all sides.
  6. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels. Serve warm.
